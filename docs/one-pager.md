# DOT Corridor Communicator
## Real-Time Multi-State Coordination for the Connected Corridor

---

### **Transform Your State's Transportation Operations**

The DOT Corridor Communicator is a **production-ready platform** serving **46 states** with real-time traffic event aggregation, intelligent analysis, and multi-state coordination capabilities. Built for State DOTs, it eliminates data silos and enables seamless interstate collaboration.

---

## **What Your State Gets Today**

### **Real-Time Event Intelligence**
- **2,500+ Live Events** aggregated from 46 state DOT feeds every 5-15 minutes
- **WZDx v4.2 Compliance** - Work Zone Data Exchange standardization for connected vehicle readiness
- **Border Coordination** - Automatic identification of events requiring multi-state coordination
- **Smart Filtering** - Interstate-only view, state-specific filtering, severity-based prioritization

### **Connected Vehicle Readiness**
- **SAE J2735 TIM Conversion** - Automatic translation to Traveler Information Messages
- **CV-TIM Identification** - Commercial vehicle-relevant event flagging
- **CIFS Export** - Common Incident Feed Specification for Waze and third-party integrations
- **Multi-Format Views** - See how raw data translates to standardized formats

### **ITS Infrastructure Management**
- **5,800+ ITS Assets Tracked** - RSUs, cameras, DMS, sensors across your corridors
- **Geospatial Proximity Analysis** - Find nearby ITS equipment for any event
- **Asset Inventory** - Centralized database of connected infrastructure

### **Multi-State Collaboration**
- **Interstate Messaging** - Coordinate with neighboring states on border events
- **Corridor Briefings** - Auto-generated reports for I-35, I-70, I-80, and other major corridors
- **Shared Situational Awareness** - See what your neighbors see in real-time
